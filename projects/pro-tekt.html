About us

Hi there,

and thank you for taking the time to find out about the team behind Pro-tekt, and how this venture came about.

Our company was founded in January 2015 in the town of Ballarat Victoria, after a chance meeting between myself (Matt Penhall) and Robert Leyton, when a mutual friend suggested we should meet to discuss an idea I had been researching. The gap within the vehicle tracking space quickly turned into a business venture!

The available tracking systems were either very expensive, out of reach for the average user, or come with a complicated set of SMS commands that gave the user only basic GPS coordinates in which to try and find their asset. (Many of these also still run on the 2G network, which has been switched off here in Australia, but they are sold as 3G!)

Matt spent many years working as an Auto-Electrician on remote mine sites across the country, He wanted an efficient and instantaneous way to know if my vehicle or he's property had been compromised without finding out two weeks later when it would be too late. This happened to a colleague of his while they were on site, and the poor bloke was an emotional mess for the rest of our stint on site. You can imagine being so far from home, stress, multiple telephone calls. All we could do as colleagues was offer words of support.

This event was the final trigger for me to act on setting about investigating this project

Robert obtained his Ph.D. Investigating and performing data analytics on cybercrime data, developing new algorithms for text mining and authorship analysis. Robert also supervises several Ph.D. and Masters students at Federation University Australia. After our initial discussion, he too was interested to get involved with the project.

We set about designing Pro-tekt. A system that allows anyone to access a high end, reliable and easy to use App based vehicle tracking platform. I am so proud to tell you that with high functionality, 24/7 Live Tracking capability, and a few other clever functions for everyday use, the only question you will need to ask yourself is how many will I need?

Robert and he's employee at the time Matt Deal. started coding the platform from a ground up build, and Matt Penhall set about designing a kit that would be simple to install with innovative components that have highly intelligent functionality.

The end product speaks for itself, and is now available to you at the click of a button.

We have been fortunate enough to have many fantastic and talented people that have shared our vision and were only too happy to get involved and help us out in so many ways along this journey. It has certainly been a hectic but exciting adventure so far, to say the least. We are confident that you will love using our product as much as we do.

The Pro-tekt Team

FAQs

  • priority_highCan you hide the tracker so it can’t be seen?
    Yes you can, the device is small in size, has a plain black case, and can be hidden away in multiple locations to avoid detection.
  • powerDoes the tracker need constant power?
    Yes the device does need to be hard wired to a power source for data transmission to our servers, but it also has a built in battery that enables the device to work in the event that the power source is disconnected.
  • subscriptionsWhy do I need to pay a monthly subscription fee?
    This monthly fee covers an included monthly data SIM plan that we supply to you with the device to be able to communicate location information, the storage of your current and historical location information, and 24/7 access to this functionality via our easy to use Pro-tekt APP.
  • wb_incandescentCan I fit the tracker myself?
    Although the installation is quite simple, we strongly suggest the system be installed by a qualified Auto-electrician, Accessory installer or Authorized Agent, as we cannot guarantee that you will have selected the correct power sources to maintain the integrity of both the device itself, and the vehicle it is being fitted to. The Warranty of the device will also be Void if damaged due to incorrect power source connection.
  • account_boxCan I add another device to my account?
    You certainly can, there is no limit. Simply purchase the new device, log into your account and select the ADD DEVICE feature, enter your devices unique code, this will then be added to your device list.
  • signal_cellular_offWhat happens if there’s no phone signal?
    f you have lost phone reception, the device will continue to store location data within the device memory, and will simply upload the stored data to our server once connection is re-established.
